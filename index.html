About EcoFi

Welcome to EcoFi, your curated guide to a more sustainable lifestyle. We believe that small choices, when made by many, can create a massive positive impact on our planet.

Our mission is simple: to make sustainable living accessible and straightforward. We're not just a product finder; we're a community dedicated to eco-friendly and ethical choices.

Our Story

EcoFi was born from a simple question: "How can I be sure the products I buy are actually good for the planet?" We grew tired of confusing labels and "greenwashing." We wanted a single place where we could discover and trust eco-conscious brands.

Today, EcoFi partners with brands and scrapes the web to bring you a curated collection of products that meet our standards for sustainability, ethics, and quality. From fashion and beauty to home goods and baby care, every item on our platform is chosen with care.

Our Promise

We're committed to transparency. We do our best to research every product, looking into materials, supply chains, and company practices. We're here to help you shop with confidence, knowing you're supporting a better future.

Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have a question about a product, a suggestion for a brand we should feature, or just want to say hello, please get in touch.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do you choose products for EcoFi?

We have a rigorous selection process. We research brands based on their material sourcing, manufacturing processes, packaging, and overall commitment to ethical and sustainable practices. We look for certifications like Fair Trade, Organic, B Corp, and more.

What is "greenwashing"?

"Greenwashing" is a deceptive marketing practice where a company spends more time and money claiming to be "green" through advertising than actually implementing business practices that minimize environmental impact. We work hard to see through this and feature genuinely sustainable products.

How do I create an account?

You can create a free account by clicking the "Sign In / Sign Up" link in the user menu (the person icon 👤) in the header. This will allow you to save products to your wishlist and manage your profile.

How do I update my password?

You can change your password by navigating to your Profile page (click the 👤 icon, then "Profile"). On that page, you will find a "Change Password" form. You will need to enter your current password and your new password.

How do I delete my account?

We're sad to see you go! You can permanently delete your account by going to your Profile page. At the bottom, you will find a "Danger Zone" with a "Delete My Account" button. Please be aware that this action is permanent and cannot be reversed.
